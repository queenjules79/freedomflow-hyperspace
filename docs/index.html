What else is possible?

Freedom Flow Radical Life Coaching uses the LaChiara method to help you follow your own path to your own solution.

The LaChiara method is faith-driven, love-infused, trauma-informed, oppression-aware and somatically attuned. Click here for more information on the LaChiara Method

The LaChiara method is not like other forms of life coaching. It offers a unique approach to life coaching that is based on the idea that each person is unique and that each person has their own unique path to follow.

We are all on a journey. Sometimes we need a guide to travel with us a way, and to help us navigate the path. Julianne Fraser is a certified life coach who can help you find the way that is right for you. Part of this work is to be aware of the reactions and responses within us and to be aware of where we have choice.

In the words of Julianne

I would love to help you move through life’s waves with resilience.

Listen, notice, feel.

Notice how this practice of relating to the energy in your body can affect, shift or awaken things inside of you.

Let’s be curious together about what else is possible.

About Julianne

Julianne is a certified life coach of the LaChiara method

My personal journey with this work began when I sought to have more resilience in the face of the trauma I was experiencing. I knew that I needed to survive and I found a lifeline within the energy work of this practice. Through the activations and the process of being curious and asking questions without an agenda, I began to discover more spaciousness within myself.

I discovered that by using these tools, I could help to regulate my nervous system, so that I could function within the oppressive culture of my job and eventually be able to find work that brought me joy instead of anxiety. I understand and deeply believe that healing is a journey, with twists and turns in all kinds of weather. I also believe that within each of us lies the same kind of power that flows within water, that gives water its power to smooth rocks and cut through earth and forge new pathways.
