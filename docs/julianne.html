Personal testimonial to the LaChiara method

My personal journey with this work began when I sought to have more resilience in the face of the trauma I was experiencing. I knew that I needed to survive and I found a lifeline within the energy work of this practice. Through the activations and the process of being curious and asking questions without an agenda, I began to discover more spaciousness within myself.

I discovered that by using these tools, I could help to regulate my nervous system, so that I could function within the oppressive culture of my job and eventually be able to find work that brought me joy instead of anxiety. I understand and deeply believe that healing is a journey, with twists and turns in all kinds of weather. I also believe that within each of us lies the same kind of power that flows within water, that gives water its power to smooth rocks and cut through earth and forge new pathways.
